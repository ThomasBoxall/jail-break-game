About

So whats this all about then...

JailBreak is a Choose Your Own Path (CYOP) adventure game based around escaping from a fictional prison off the south cost of Portsmouth.

The game was originally developed for the Pint of Science School of Criminology and Criminal Justice event as a time-filler activity but it turned into much more!
